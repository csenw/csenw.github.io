Currently, I am a lecturer in School of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering (ITEE) in the University of Queensland (UQ). I am also an adjunct senior research fellow in Institute for Integrated and Intelligent Systems (IIIS) in Griffith University (GU). Previously, I worked as a lecture of computer science in School of Information and Communication Technology (ICT) in GU from July 2016 to January 2019. I received my PhD degree in UQ in 2014. Between Jan 2014 and July 2016, I have been an ARC post-doctoral research fellow in UQ.

My research interest includes various topics on Feature Selection, Semi-supervised Learning, Deep Learning, Pattern Recognition, and Data Mining. Also, I have always been concentrating on significant applications using AI and Data Mining techniques, such as health informatics and social media analysis. My CV can be found at here.
